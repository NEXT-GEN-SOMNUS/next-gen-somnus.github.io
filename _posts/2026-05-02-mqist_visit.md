The students of the Master in Quantum Information, Sciences and Technologies (MQIST) traveled to A Coruña from Santiago and Vigo to enjoy a day together. The morning passed at the CITIC facilities in the new ICT City of A Coruña. 

There they were able to learn first-hand about the projects that are being developed in quantum computing in this Singular Center as well as in the Faculty of Informatics of the UDC. Late in the morning, ITG (Technological Institute of Galicia), also presented the projects it develops in the field of quantum computing applied to communications and energy networks.
