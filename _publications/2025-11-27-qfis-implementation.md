title: "Quantum Fuzzy Inference Systems: Implementation and a Case Study on Sleep Apnea Detection"
author: Magaz-Romero, Samuel; Mosqueira-Rey, Eduardo; Alvarez-Estevez, Diego; Moret-Bonillo, Vicente
corresponding: s.magazr@udc.es
description: We present a new method for implementing FIS with quantum circuits, named Quantum Fuzzy Inference Systems, providing the definitions for the logical operators and implication. We propose a practical application in sleep medicine diagnosis, more specifically for the detection of apneic events. Our results show that we are able to replicate the behavior of the classical model using the quantum proposal, up to a similarity of 99.97%.
